VoiceOver Quick Guide

  • ⌘+F5 to toggle on/off
  • Ctrl + Alt + Shift + Down Arrow to enter a content block
  • Ctrl + Alt + Left/Right Arrow to navigate items
  • Ctrl to pause/resume while speaking
